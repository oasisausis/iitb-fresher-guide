What to tell the operator

  1. Your exact location: building, hostel, floor, room or nearest landmark.
  2. What happened and whether the danger is continuing.
  3. How many people are injured or at risk.
  4. Your name and callback number.
  5. Stay on the line and follow instructions.

While help is coming

  • Move away from fire, traffic, electricity, chemicals or other immediate hazards.
  • Ask someone to guide responders from the nearest road or entrance.
  • Do not move a seriously injured person unless remaining there is dangerous.
  • For emotional crisis or risk of self-harm, call 112/QRT or IITB Hospital and seek immediate in-person help.
