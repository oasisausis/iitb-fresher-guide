How to get to IIT Bombay

Trajectory Optimization: Minimizing Travel Time (t)

⚠️ Crucial Main Gate Access Notice

Before leaving your hometown, check your inbox for the official onboarding email sent by the Director/Academic Office. This email contains a unique barcode/QR scanner pass. Until your permanent IIT Bombay physical ID card is generated, security personnel will require you to present this digital pass at the main gate for verification on your very first arrival.

🚂 Arriving by Train

If your long-distance train terminates at one of Mumbai's primary outstation hubs:

  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (CSMT)
  • Lokmanya Tilak Terminus (LTT) - Very close to Powai
  • Dadar Central (DR) / Mumbai Central (MMCT)
  • Bandra Terminus (BDTS) / Borivali (BVI)
The Local Sequence: Use the Central Line local train network to reach Kanjurmarg Railway Station (the closest rail checkpoint to campus). From Kanjurmarg West, step out to the auto stand and hail a share or meter auto-rickshaw directly to the IIT Bombay Main Gate.
📱 Download m-Indicator App (Essential for Local Trains)

✈️ Arriving by Flight

Landing at Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (BOM) Terminal 1 or Terminal 2:

The Metro Vector: Board the Mumbai Metro network and make your way toward the Aarey Joint Venture Link Road (JVLR) station.
The Final Leg: Once you alight at Aarey JVLR, head downstairs to the street level. You can easily pick up a direct auto-rickshaw down the JVLR corridor straight to the campus entrance.

🚖 The Direct Vector: Prepaid & App Cabs

If you are carrying heavy luggage (books, mattresses, or instrumentation), you can bypass local transit transformations entirely. Book an Uber, Ola, or a prepaid airport/station taxi directly to IIT Bombay, Powai. Instruct the driver to head toward the Main Gate via JVLR or Powai Lake road.
