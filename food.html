⚡ Quick Bites & Snacks

KReSIT Building Canteen (Ground Floor)

When you need to grab something fast between heavy coding or physics sessions, this is the spot. They serve quick snacks like hot tea, vada pav etc.

Chea Canteen (Chemistry Dept, 1st Floor)

Conveniently located for science students. Drop by for a rapid refuel of tea, vada pav, puffs, and fresh sandwiches before heading back to the lab.
